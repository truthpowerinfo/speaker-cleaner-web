How to use

When your phone drop in toilet, sink, drain or pool & you need to get that water out, then you can use to release the app. Any kind of liquid can damage your device. for the best results, Place the device's speaker facing the ground, Turn the volume up to maximum.


Science behind it.

Sound waves propagate by vibrating particles. When there’s water in your Device’s speaker, it’s actually a miniscule amount that covers the membrane and obstructs it from operating normally. That’s why your device speaker sounds muffled because it has a layer of water and to break the water layer into individual droplets, the speaker needs to vibrate so that it can literally shake the water off.
